About Abhaya Bikram Shahi

A Junior Web Developer with a Vision to Transform Nepal's Digital Landscape

Abhaya Bikram Shahi

Abhaya Bikram Shahi is a 14-year-old junior web developer from Nepal with a passion for technology and entrepreneurship. At a young age, he founded **Abhaya Solutions**, a company focused on delivering high-quality web development and design solutions. His goal is to enhance the digital presence of businesses and individuals by utilizing his expertise in web technologies.

Abhaya's skills span HTML, CSS, and web development frameworks. Despite his young age, his proficiency and dedication have made him a rising star in the Nepali tech community. Currently, he is contributing to **Badimalika Secondary School**, where he works on designing user-friendly apps and websites for the school.
